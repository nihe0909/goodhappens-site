Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my entries across devices?

Good Happens uses iCloud to sync your data automatically. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID on all devices and that iCloud is enabled for Good Happens: go to Settings → Apple ID → iCloud and check that Good Happens is turned on.

Is my journal data private?

Yes, completely. Your entries are stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not have servers and cannot access your data. We use no analytics, no tracking, and no ads.

How do I create a Good Things Book?

Open Good Happens, go to the Book section, select a date range, and the app will generate a beautifully designed journal from your entries. You can export it as a PDF to print, keep, or share with loved ones.

Can I export my journal entries?

You can share individual entries or export a Good Things Book as a beautifully designed PDF. Your data is also automatically backed up through iCloud if enabled.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the VIP / Premium section in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

The app is not syncing. What should I do?

Try these steps: (1) Make sure you have a stable internet connection. (2) Check that iCloud is enabled for Good Happens in your device Settings. (3) Try closing and reopening the app. (4) If the issue persists, sign out of iCloud and sign back in.

Which devices are supported?

Good Happens works on iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, Mac (Apple Silicon), and Apple Vision Pro. All devices sync through iCloud when enabled.

How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Good Happens follows your system appearance by default. You can also choose from multiple skins within the app's settings to customize the look to your preference.
